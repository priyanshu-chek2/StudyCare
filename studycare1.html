About Study Care

Study Care is a thoughtfully designed application that helps students maintain a healthy balance between focused study sessions and restorative breaks.

Using the proven Pomodoro Technique, our app alternates between study periods and breaks to maximize concentration while preventing burnout.

Unlike other productivity apps, Study Care emphasizes wellness as much as productivity, offering health tips and reminders to keep both mind and body in optimal condition during long study sessions.

Our Mission

We believe that sustainable learning requires caring for both mental and physical health. Our mission is to help students:

  • Develop healthy study habits
  • Prevent burnout and eye strain
  • Maintain focus during study sessions
  • Improve long-term knowledge retention
  • Balance productivity with self-care

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Pomodoro Technique work?
The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. After several sessions, you take a longer break. This approach helps maintain focus and prevents mental fatigue.
Can I customize the study and break durations?
Yes! Study Care allows you to fully customize your study sessions (5-60 minutes), short breaks (1-30 minutes), and long breaks (5-60 minutes). You can also set how many study sessions occur before a long break.
Is my data stored or tracked?
Study Care respects your privacy. All timer data and settings are stored locally on your device. We don't collect or transmit any personal information.
Is Study Care completely free to use?
Yes, Study Care is completely free with no hidden costs or premium tiers. We believe wellness tools should be accessible to all students.
